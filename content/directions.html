Directions

MuniHac is held at the premises of TNG Technology Consulting GmbH in Unterföhring (Munich).

Location

TNG Technology Consulting GmbH
 Betastraße 13a
85774 Unterföhring

By rail (both from city and airport)

Take S-Bahn line S8, exit at the stop Unterföhring, leave the station heading southwards, after 200 m turn left (to the East) towards the Heinrich-Hildebrand footpath, follow it for about 800 m.

By bus

Take line 233, exit at stop Betastraße, or take line 189, exit at stop Dieselstraße. You can plan your trip via mvv-muenchen.de.

By car

It's usually not too hard to find a parking spot. See the map above.
