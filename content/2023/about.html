About MuniHac

MuniHac is an annual hackathon and conference in Munich that brings together Haskell developers and enthusiasts from all over the world.

It takes place over three days and will coincide with Big Techday 23. On Friday, July 7th, all MuniHac participants are invited to attend TNG’s conference on science and IT at Motorworld Munich for a day of talks by world-class speakers and exciting exhibits. On Saturday, July 8th and Sunday, July 9th, MuniHac will feature a diverse range of workshops, talks, and projects for both beginners and experienced developers at TNG’s office located at Beta-Straße 13 in Unterföhring. Furthermore, there is an optional beginners workshop on Thursday, July 6th before the official start of the event.

Join us for free for a unique chance to improve your Haskell skills, network with other experts in the community and attend two of Munich’s longest running IT and science conferences. If you are unable to take part in person, you can re-watch most talks on YouTube after the event.
