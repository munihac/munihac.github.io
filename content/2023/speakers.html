Confirmed Speakers

Christiaan Baaij

QBayLogic

Christiaan is co-founder at QBayLogic where he and the rest of the team build custom FPGA solutions for a worldwide clientele. Christiaan's journey into Haskell started in 2009 when he together with another student built the first version of the Clash compiler, a compiler from Haskell to hardware.

Aside from the Clash project itself, Christiaan also maintains various GHC plugins for type-level natural numbers that have found their use outside of the Clash project.
