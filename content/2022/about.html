About MuniHac

We're glad to announce that MuniHac will be back this year, as an on-site event in Munich.

MuniHac is a three-day Haskell hackathon and conference hosted in the beautiful city of Munich. It's aimed at both Haskell beginners and experts, featuring both a beginners track and talks from Haskell experts from all over the world. And it's free (as in beer)!

Registration will open soon. Follow us on Twitter or subscribe to our newsletter so you don't miss anything!
