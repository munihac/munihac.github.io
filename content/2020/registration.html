Registration

Registration for MuniHac 2020 has closed. If you still want to join last minute, you can register on Slack and have a look at the #munihac-2020 channel.

Projects

Although the focus this year is more on workshops and talks, MuniHac would not be a proper Haskell hackathon without some hacking sessions. We've reserved a few Zoom rooms for you to get together and hack on projects, so if you want to host a project at MuniHac 2020, just get in touch with us on Slack in the #support channel! We'll create a Zoom session and Slack channel for you, and publish them so people can join you for your project.
